Privacy Policy

Welcome to TubeDLP. I am committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, transparent service.

Information Collection

TubeDLP operates as a simple video downloading service and collects minimal personal information:

  • Video URLs that you input for downloading
  • Basic usage data (like browser type and access time) for service improvement
  • No account creation is required or available

How Information is Used

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Processing your video download requests
  • Improving service performance and reliability
  • Maintaining service security

Data Storage

TubeDLP does not store any downloaded videos or personal information. All downloads are processed in real-time and delivered directly to your device.

Third-Party Services

The service interacts with YouTube to process download requests. Users should be aware of and comply with YouTube's terms of service when using TubeDLP.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Know what data is being collected
  • Request deletion of any stored data
  • Opt-out of future communications

Contact Information

For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact me through the "Contact Me" button in the navigation bar or via the WhatsApp link in the footer.
